instructions

Step 1:

Take a book and keep it aside. Keep the screen (preferrably computer) at an arms length distance and cover one of your eye with your hand.

Step 2:

Read all the letters and write them in your book per row. Click on the small replica image of the eyechart you used below.

Step 3:

Once you've clicked on your respective eyechart, put the letters you wrote in your book per row in the text boxes and then click on submit. You'll receive your results via a small pop-up box.
